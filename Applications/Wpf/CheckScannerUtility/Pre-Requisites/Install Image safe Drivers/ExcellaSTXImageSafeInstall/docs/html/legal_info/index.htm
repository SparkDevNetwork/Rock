How Customer Information is Collected, Retained, and Used

We gather information from the following sources:

  • Information we receive from you on applications or other forms.
  • Information about your transaction with us, or others; and
  • Information we receive from consumer reporting agencies.

The information collected is only used to process your order internally, to ship the product you have ordered, and to follow up on customer service issues.

Accurate Information About You
Should you ever believe that our records contain inaccurate or incomplete information about you, please contact us at (888) 624-8350.We will investigate your concerns and take appropriate action in a timely manner.

We Will Limit Employee Access to Your Information at MagTek
We safeguard information according to established security standards and procedures that comply with Federal standards. Employee access to non-public customer information is limited to those who need to know in order to provide products or services to you. Our employees are educated on the importance of complying with this privacy policy.

Restrictions on Disclosing Your Personal Information
We release account and other personal information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of MagTek, our customers, or others. In the case of bad checks, unauthorized credit card use, etc, the information will be provided to law enforcement agencies and others as appropriate.

Maintaining Your Privacy in Business Relationships with Third Parties
Information about our customers is an important part of our business, and we are not in the business of selling it to others. We do not sell, rent, or share your address, email address, fax, or phone with third-party companies for commercial purposes. We may employ other companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. Examples include delivering packages, sending postal mail and e-mail, processing credit card payments, and providing customer service. They have access to personal information needed to perform their functions, but may not use it for other purposes.

Ending Customer Relationships
We will treat the information we have about you as if you were still our customer, if your customer relationship ends.

Protecting Your Privacy
In order to protect your privacy and comply with the law, additional information may be requested, when you inquire about your accounts.

This may appear to be inconvenient, but protecting your personal information is important to us.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact MagTek at privacy@magtek.com or call our Help Desk at (888) 624-8350.
