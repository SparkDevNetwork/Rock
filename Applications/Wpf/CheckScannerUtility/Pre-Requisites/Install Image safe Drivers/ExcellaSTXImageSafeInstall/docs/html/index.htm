Check Reading Documentation

MagTek's MICR check readers and scanners meet and exceed the requirements for all electronic check applications, including check conversion and Check 21. Daily, thousands of retailers and financial institutions worldwide use our check readers and scanners to reduce transaction time and processing costs, reading billions of real-world checks with a high degree of MICR accuracy and excellent image quality. MagTek's MICR product line assures complete confidence in data captured from checks, credit cards and driver's licenses.
