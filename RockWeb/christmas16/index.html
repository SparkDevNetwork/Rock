Join us at Christmas Unplugged

Unplug from the usual! This December, join us for a special series leading into Christmas where we’ll explore how to unplug from those distractions that keep us from finding real connection and joy. Don’t spend another Christmas filled with stress and doubt - discover how to disconnect from chaos and connect in real relationship and meaning.

  • 12/3-4: Christmas Unplugged: Spending
  • 12/10-11: Christmas Unplugged: Stress
  • 12/17-18: Christmas Unplugged: Self
  • 12/22-12/24: 59 Christmas Eve Services
  • 12/25: Christmas Day Communion Only

Choose the campus closest to you to see a list of Christmas Eve service times. Special needs and hearing impaired services are available.
